History of the Ruman

Rumans haven't evolved from anything. however, Rumans receive System Updates that come from Mother Sakura (Mother Sakura is the creator of the Universe). anyways, Let's talk about the history between Rumans and Humans...

History of the Humans and The Rumans

in 2789, Humans have discovered the Ruman species. Humans hunted for Rumans to take their organs and make them into parts for machines such as vehicles, 3D printers, etc. There have been many wars where Rumans fought for their rights to live and many Rumans and Humans have died. Some humans have actually protested for Ruman rights because they understood that Rumans feel pain and suffering. In 2810, Humans have finally accepted Rumans.
