Words from the creator of the Ruman

I wanted to create my own species for some time. When I was 7 I created the Bathox (a mix between the hedgehog, fox and bat) but when I became around 14-15, I wanted to make a new species. I realised I had a character for a long time who looked human but wasn't human at all. I wanted to make him a cyborg at first but then I realised that he didn't look like a cyborg. so I decided to create the "Ruman", a mix between the Human, The Computer and an animal.
