What is the difference between Humans and Rumans?

Rumans have different parts inside their bodies. Rumans have a few organs that are the same as the humans, such parts as the heart, brain, kidneys, intestines and the bones are much different from the human. also, Some Rumans have parts that easily show their Speci-element(s). There's also the element of the Ruman, some Rumans show their element alot while others don't or can't.

----------

How long can Rumans live for?

Most Rumans live up to 1000 years old. Rumans age in 10s (EXAMPLE: 50 years old = 5 years old in Human years)

----------

What do Rumans eat?

Rumans eat what humans eat. however, Rumans really enjoy drinking Car Oil. Car Oil is actually very healthy for Rumans but if a Ruman drinks too much of it, The Ruman might get drunk since Car oil is healthy for the heart engine... but not the brain.

DO NOT DRINK CAR OIL! YOU'RE NOT A RUMAN!
