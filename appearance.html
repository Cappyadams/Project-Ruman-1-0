Appearance of Rumans

The apperarance of a ruman really depends on the Speci-element and element of the Ruman. but most Rumans look extremely human, others look like an animal with a humanroid skeleton. Some Rumans might have animal legs, wings, tails, tentacle hair, etc! A Ruman can look like anything as long as the Ruman has a humanroid skeleton!
